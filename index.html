Meet the Sola Compiler,

a step forward in software development education.

The Sola Compiler is an application designed to compile Sola, a brand new programming language. Our goal is to bring Sola to an educational environment. We want to empower educators to teach students fundamental concepts of programming, with minimal costs to their institutions.

We’re designing Sola to be easier to use, and learn, than most major programming languages, and that makes it perfect to use as a tool to allow young programmers to understand the logic behind the code that they write.

Sola isn’t available just yet, as we’re still testing and developing our language, but you can join us here to follow our progress. We have a lot planned for the future, we’re looking forward to sharing that with every update!
